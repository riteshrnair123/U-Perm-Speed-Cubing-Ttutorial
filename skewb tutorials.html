How to Solve a Skewb

Steps for solving a skewb:

Step 1. Solve the white corner

For the white sticker on the front side, do the algorithm in the left side and for the white sticker facing the right side, do the algorithm on the right side. These algorithms are supper easy and can be learned fast.

Step 2. Solve the yellow corners

Do the algorithm according to the different positions of the yellow corners, then solve it to the opposite side of the white face

This move is called: SLEDGE HAMMER

Step 3. Solve the yellow face

Solve the yellow face

Adjust the position of the yellow center, do the algorithm to solve it to the face of the yellow corner.

Placement: Place the white face on the front left and the yellow center piece on the left back.

Step 4. Solve the Skewb

Observe the solved faces and do the algorithm according to below following cases, then the Skewb is solved.

Cas A: Only the yellow and white faces are solved.

Case B: The yellow, white and another faces are solved.

OR

Congradulation on solving your first skewb.
